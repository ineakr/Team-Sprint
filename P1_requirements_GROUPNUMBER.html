The client

  • Client name: Team Sprint
  • Contact person: Arne Skuterud & Carina Jagtøyen

Our client, Team Sprint, is a team of four Norwegian speed skaters. We were introduced to this client by Carina, who is one of the speed skaters in Team Sprint. As the name of the team implies, the four athletes specialize in the sprint distances of speed skating, which are 100, 500 and 1000 meters.

Purpose and goals

Because of the Norwegian tradition of long-distance speed skating, Team Sprint seeks to educate and inspire Norwegian coaches and young speed skaters into wanting to specialize in sprint. Our goal to fulfill this purpose is to create a platform for Team Sprint to share their knowledge about sprint training, in the form of training programs, exercises and thoughts about sprint technique. In other words, we need to provide users with the opportunity to watch videos of the various exercises that are mentioned in the training programs. Another goal, to fulfill the purpose of inspiring, is to give a short introduction of each athlete, a description of Team Sprint´s work and goals and provide updates from competitions and training camps.

Audience

The main audience of this website will be coaches and athletes from different speed skating clubs and teams in Norway. This group will use the website to learn about sprint technique and to find inspiration in exercises that contribute to improve the attributes that are essential for sprint on ice. The audience also consists of those who are interested in following the journey and results of the Team Sprint athletes.

Content og the website and Organization

  1. “About Us”

    This section will introduce Team Sprint to the users through a short description of their work and their goals. Additionally, each of the athletes and coaches will be presented with a portrait, a few words about themselves and a list of their personal records. This page will provide other speed skaters, coaches, supporters, and potential sponsors with the opportunity to get to know Team Sprint on a deeper level.

  2. “Updates and Results”
  3. “Training Philosophy”
    • “Programs”
    • “Exercises”
    • “Technique”
  4. “Sponsors”
  5. “Facebook and Instagram”

Footnote: Every page of the website may include a footnote with Team Sprints contact information and links to their Facebook and Instagram account. We may also create a section below the contact information that will contain the logos of Team Sprints sponsors. This will help Team Sprint promote their sponsors furthermore and give potential sponsors the opportunity to contact Team Sprint directly.
